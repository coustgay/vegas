FRIDAY EVENING

The drive to Vegas is long, so you spend Friday night in the desert.

After setting up camp, you all sit around the campfire and discuss how to spend the evening.

$1: I have this bottle that I brought labelled "Whiskey", but I'm not sure if I trust it. Anyone care to help me test it?

$2: I brought a blacklight, we could go look for scorpions! They glow neon green under UV!

$3: I brought cards, do you want to practice for tomorrow with some classic war?

$4: God, I've been eyeing that mountain all night, I'm just itching to get to the top of it.

You consider your options, knowing that you will only be able to join one of them
